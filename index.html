Experiences

Standard

When going to the movies is all that matters to you, our standard experience is all you need. There's your usual cinema seating closer to the screen, or comfier Standard Plus seats near the back, and you get a choice of classic cinema snacks to enjoy the movie with.

Junior

Treat your child to their best ever trip to the movies in the cinema designed just for them. They'll love slouching back in the quirky beanbag seats, with a choice of special kiddie combo meals to keep them well-fed.

Dolby

Turn your trip to the movies into a captivating, cinematic event, with state-of-the-art image and sound technology from Dolby immersing you in the majestic sights and epic sounds of every scene.
